About Me

Konichiwa! Jain Tarun desu.

I am Tarun Jain, currently working as a Data Scientist at AI Planet, a Belgium-based AI startup with over 2+ years experience. I was also part of Google Summer of Code 2024 at Red Hen Lab. I have been organizing workshops and AI/ML talks for over 2.5+ years as a Pie & AI Bangalore Ambassador at DeepLearningAI. This eventually led to the path of becoming a GDE in ML. I joined the Google Developer Experts program in October 2023. In my free time, I watch Anime and read Manga- One Piece being my favorite.
